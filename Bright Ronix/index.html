ABOUT US

BRIGHTRONIX is a reliable and registered startup company, which was established in the year 2018, located at Buldhana, Maharashtra, India. We are inspired by the “Make in India” initiative by our government and strongly support "Be Vocal about Local" pledge which means आत्मनिर्भर भारत. BRIGHTRONIX specializes in the research and production of varieties of smart LED lighting products for households, markets and public places taking "high quality, efficiency, service, stability, and energy saving".

The company is glad for its convention of conveying predominant quality both in our items and also our client’s benefits.

VISION

BRIGHTRONIX strives to be one of the eco-friendly global leaders for Lighting & Electronics solutions to our customers.

Quality Assurance

In developing products & services, quality assurance is any systematic process of checking to see whether a product or service being developed is meeting specified requirements or not. We have a separate department devoted to quality assurance. Our quality assurance plan is to increase customer confidence and satisfactions. The main aim is to improve work processes and efficiency, and to enable us to better compete with others.

Our quality assurance systems emphasize catching defects before they get into the final product. Our engineers who has wide experience and has carried out a number of tests for many third parties conduct quality test of our range.
